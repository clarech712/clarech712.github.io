Klára Churá

Incoming senior in CS and Math @ Tufts.

During the Summer of 2024, I am a Quant Analyst Intern at Imperative Execution.

Until mid-June, I was a visiting student in Computer Science at the University of Oxford, where I was lucky to work on super exciting research under Jakob Foerster at FLAIR.

In the upcoming final year of my BSc, I will be writing my senior honors thesis under Jivko Sinapov at the MuLIP Lab.

After graduating in May 2025, I hope to remain in academia as a PhD student in Computer Science. I am broadly interested in artificial intelligence, especially reinforcement learning, robotics, multi-agent systems, and game theory, so that is what I am likely to pursue further!

In my free time, I am a polyglot bookworm, injury-prone runner, and avid cafe journaler.
